Darcy McDonald (b. 2000) is a Sydney-based recording artist, photographer, videographer & poet. McDonald grew up in Sydney’s Inner West, where at age 19, he left to study Jazz Performance at The New School for Jazz & Contemporary Music in New York City, receiving Honors at the completion of his studies & working as Assistant Engineer at If & Only If Studios, NYC.

With an experimental pragmatism and a devotion to analog technology — McDonald draws a single, distinct thread in his work, creating narratives that explore unbounded ecological perspectives on youth, nature, grief & humanness rooted in our present, everyday realities.

His work was featured at the 25th Annual MATA Festival in New York City, and he has toured extensively across the U.S. & Australia being featured at National Sawdust, NuBlu, Mercury Lounge, Arlene’s Grocery, Lazybones & countless other venues.
